S Y N O P S I S

Mycelium, the microbial foundation for mushrooms, possesses many advantageous properties including adaptaptivity and ease of growth. We at MuGen seek to develop specific strains of mycelium that are more efficient, faster growing, and capable of consuming non-biological materials. As a company that values sustainability, we wish to develop these mycelium strains to address both current and future resource challenges.
